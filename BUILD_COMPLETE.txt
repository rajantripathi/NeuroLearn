🎯 KEY FEATURES

Adaptive Tone System:
  • Focused → Concise, task-oriented
  • Overwhelmed → Calm, step-by-step
  • Distracted → Brief refocus
  • Neutral → Warm, encouraging

Focus Sprint System:
  • 5-25 minute timed sessions
  • Live countdown timer
  • Progress tracking
  • Success/failure logging

RAG-Powered Support:
  • Evidence-based ADHD strategies
  • Context-aware retrieval
  • Academic source citations
  • 6 strategy categories

Document Support:
  • PDF/DOCX/TXT upload
  • ADHD-friendly summarization
  • Reading time estimates
  • Key concept extraction

Privacy & Ethics:
  • 100% local data storage
  • No cloud logging
  • Diagnostic query rejection
  • Clear medical disclaimer
